
CS472-Homework-01-HTML
1. Identify block vs. inline HTML elements: <div>, <span>, <p>, <h1/6>, <hr>, <br>, <img>, <a>, <ol>/<li>, <ul>/<li>, <table>/<tr>/<th>/<td>, <form>, <input>, <textarea>, <button>, <select>/<option>, <label>.

Answer:

Block Elements:
- <div>
- <p>
- <h1> to <h6>
- <hr>
- <ol>
- <ul>
- <li>
- <table>
- <tr>
- <th>
- <td>
- <form>

Inline Elements:
- <span>
- <br>
- <img>
- <a>
- <input>
- <textarea>
- <button>
- <select>
- <option>
- <label>
